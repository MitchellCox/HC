Document Change Emails

If you have opted to get notifications of updates to your documents, you can turn them off by following the steps below.

  1. Open the document in question
  2. Click File>Set Reminder
  3. Un-click the box that says remind me every [7] days to update this document
Notifications of Requests to Join Team

If you're a team administrator and you would not like to be notified of users' requests to join your team, follow the steps below:

  1. Log into Lucidchart (assure you're logging in on the team administrator account)
  2. Open the Team Page under Collaborators
  3. Uncheck "Receive email notification about request to join team"
