Educational Uses:

Lucid Software offers free educational accounts to teachers and students, with full access to Lucidchart’s premium features. Lucidchart is a powerful tool to help teachers create lesson plans, and aid students with brainstorms, concept maps, and group work. Visual learners can use Lucidchart to easily see how concepts are connected and related. Venn diagrams, curriculum flows, and entity relationship diagrams are just a few Lucidchart features to will help you revolutionize your classroom learning.

Features Included:

Educational accounts include all the benefits of a paid Pro or Team account, meaning you have access to all the features Lucidchart offers.

Requesting an Educator Account

  1. If you are a new user, sign up for a free trial using your educational email address. We offer automatic upgrades to email addresses that end in:
    • edu
    • k12
    • ac
  2. If you already have an account, log in and make sure the email address listed is your educational email address. To change your email, visit “Account” > ”User Settings”.
  3. Navigate to theEducation Page and click “Get the Free Upgrade”.
  4. Complete the form, and you will receive a confirmation email once your upgrade has been approved.

Requesting a Student Account

  1. If you are a new user, sign up for a free trial using your educational email address. We offer automatic upgrades to email addresses that end in:
    • .edu
    • .k12
    • .ac
  2. If you already have an account, log in and make sure the email address listed is your educational email address. To change your email, visit “Account” > “User Settings”.
  3. On your Account page, click the “Get your free educational upgrade” button on the right.
  4. Follow the instructions on that page by sharing Lucidchart with 3 friends.
  5. Click “Send Confirmation Email”.
  6. Follow the link sent to you to confirm your email address. Your account will automatically be upgraded.

Adding Users to a Team:

  1. Once your account is approved, you will receive an email with a custom URL to share with your students or other faculty members. When users register through this URL, they will automatically be added to your account.
  2. You can also invite students and other faculty members to join from the Team Management page of your account.

Transferring Admin Role:

If you need to transfer the admin role, the main account admin can visit our Transfer Page and make another team member the new owner of the account.

Adding Licenses:

If you need more licenses, please submit a request for the number of users you would like added to your account.

Renewing:

Educational accounts need to be renewed annually. Before your subscription ends, you’ll receive an email reminding you to submit a renewal request.
