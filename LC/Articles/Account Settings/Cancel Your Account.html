We’re sad to see you go! To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

  1. Click your username in the top right corner of the screen
  2. Select “Account Settings” from the drop down menu
  3. Click the “Close Account” tab on the left
  4. Select “Cancel Your Subscription”

After cancellation, your account will automatically downgrade to a Free Account. You will still have “View-Only” access to your documents, and you can resubscribe at any time.

(Psst… before you go: Check out our Help Center and Community to find answers to frequently asked questions.
