About

Lucidchart Product Forums allow you to collaborate with a community of other users to answer Lucidchart product questions. Quickly find and respond to questions about Lucidchart products while contributing to discussions with people around the world.

We are looking for Top Contributors who will foster an engaging community by answering community posts of other users. Top Contributors act as an integral member of our Lucidchart team by answering questions and helping resolve the issues of millions of users.   

Benefits

Top Contributors will be recognized in the Community Help Forum with a "Top Contributor" logo next to their name. Additionally, Top Contributors will have the opportunity to join a community of people who love to share knowledge and learn about Lucid products through private Top Contributor hangouts, meetups, and beta testing programs. Top Contributors will have direct access to the Lucidchart Product Team for help with questions or feedback.  Most importantly, Top Contributors will receive Lucid swag and will have the opportunity to work directly with our team on community strategy.
