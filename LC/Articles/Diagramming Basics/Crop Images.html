With Lucidchart, you can crop an image in seconds.

  1. You can access the crop option one of three ways.
    • Right click on the image. Select “Edit Mask.”
    • From the menu bar, select “Edit” then “‘Edit Mask.”
    • From right toolbar, open the Graphic panel. Select “Edit Mask.”

  2. Four black marks will appear in the corners of the photo. Drag them to crop the image.

  3. Press Enter to save the crop, or press Escape to exit the crop mode without saving changes.
  4. Changing the size or orientation of the image does not affect the crop. The crop is also non-destructive, so you can restore or adjust the image cropping at any time.
