The Image Manager makes it easy to add images to your documents directly from your computer and/or social media. This tutorial explains how to upload an image, connect to Facebook and other social media, find images using image search integrations and keep your imported images organized.

Uploading an image

Images that you drag or copy and paste onto the canvas will be saved to your image library. You can also upload images directly via the Image Manager. To do so, follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Image Manager. You can do this by clicking on More Shapes, turning on User Images, and selecting [+] from the User Images window OR select “Window” from the top menu, then “Images.”

  2. Once in the Image Manager, click the large blue “Upload” button in the top left corner.

  3. Select an image file from your computer, and click “open.”

  4. Click on the image you want to add to your document.
  5. Edit image as needed.
Upload images from Flickr, Facebook, or Dropbox
  1. Open the image manager.
  2. Select the image source on the left column of the image manager.
  3. Press ‘Connect’ in the bottom right corner.
  4. Sign in to your account.
  5. Select photos you wish to import.
  6. Your images should now be added to your Image Manager

 Note: Images imported from Visio files will be available under the ‘Visio Imports’ tab.  For help importing from Visio, please see our tutorial here.



Finding images in Bing Search or Icon Finder
  1. Select either “Bing Search” or “Icon Finder”
  2. Enter a search query into the available search bar and press enter.
  3. Select the images you wish to import.
  4. Your images should now appear in your Image Manager.

Organizing your images

Lucidchart allows you to delete, archive and privatize images, add tags and name your images, and share images with your team.

Select or deselect images

  1. To select or deselect an image, check or uncheck the box in the upper left corner of the image.
  2. To select or deselect all images, check or uncheck the box in the upper right corner of the Image Manager.

Delete images

When you delete an image, it will be removed from all documents and Image Libraries where it is in use, including those of your teammates.

  1. Select the images to be deleted.
  2. Click on the trash bin icon in the upper tool bar.
  3. Press “Delete” on the pop-up message.

Archive images

Archive Images that are still being used on documents, but that you want out of the way in your Image Manager. Archived images will be removed from the “All Images” and “Team Images” tabs, but will be available under “Archived Images” and any other previously designated tabs.

  1. Select the images to be archived.
  2. Click the ‘Archive’ icon on the top bar.

Privatize images

Making images private will prevent them from appearing under “Team Images.” By default, all imported images are available to your team members. To make an image private, follow the steps listed below.

  1. Select the images you wish to make private.
  2. Open the tag manager
  3. Select the “Private” tag
  4. An orange tag should be visible in the upper right corner of the image.

Managing image tags

Image tags allow you to organize and sort your images. This is a useful feature when working with a large number of images.

Add images to existing tags

  1. Select images to be tagged.
  2. Press the “Tags” icon on the top bar.
  3. Select or deselect desired tags.

Create a new tag

  1. From the left toolbar or in tag manager, select “Create new tag…”.
  2. Enter tag name.
  3. Select tag nesting (use nesting if the tag is a subgroup under another tag).
  4. Press “Ok.”

Hotkeys for image tags

  1. Open tag view: ‘t’
  2. Close tag view: ‘Esc’
  3. Navigate: arrow up and down
  4. Select/Deselect: spacebar
  5. Create new tag: navigate to bottom (“Create new tag...”) and press Enter

Name your images

  1. Hover over an image.
  2. Click on the i in the bottom right corner of the image.

  3. Click on the current image name.
  4. Enter the new image name.
  5. Press “Ok.”

Share Images with your team
  1. ‘Team images’ is a tag automatically shared by all images uploaded by members of your team.
  2. Team images are marked with a blue banner on the top, right corner.
  3. You can turn team images on and off by using the button in the bottom, left corner of the image manager.

Note: Turning team images off will stop team images from appearing under any tag other than ‘Team Images,’ so they will no longer appear under ‘All Images’.  This also hides all Team tags from the image manager. Team images are all the images your team members have uploaded (and haven’t made private), and will be marked with a ‘Team Image’ tag.
