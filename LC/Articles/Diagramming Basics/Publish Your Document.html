Start sharing your document by choosing an option from the Share dialogue


To share a document, click on the blue hamburger button in the top right of the document. Click on 'Share' to open the Share dialogue.

 

With Collaborators

  1.  Click "With collaborators" and enter the email address or group name you would like to invite.

  2. Select a permission level with the drop down on the gray button, and enter an optional message to accompany your invitation.

  3. Click the blue "Share" button.


On the web

  1.  Click "On the web" and enter a document name.

  2. Select the desired security options.
    • Allow your viewers to download a PDF of the published document.
    • Hide the Lucidpress logo from the published document viewer.
    • Include the Social Sharing option to allow viewers to share your published. document.
    • If your published document contains confidential information, set a password to only allow authorized viewers with the password to access the published document.
  3. Click the blue "Publish" button and enter a Standard or Custom link option. If you choose the custom link option, simply enter the suffix that you want in the URL, and click "Save."

  4. If you want to change the title the document is published under, update the published URL, or unpublish the link, simply click the green "Settings" button, and make your changes on the next page.


On Social Media

  1. Click "On Social Media" and select the social media app you want to use. Sign into your account in that product, and follow the prompts to post.

In Email

  1. After publishing, you can insert your document into a an email program like Constant Contact.  Select the desired thumbnail size, and click "Generate Code."

  2.  Copy and paste the code into the HTML section of your email.



On your Website

  1. Choose "On your website" to generate embed code for an iFrame or widget.



  2. Select the size you want, check out the preview, and click the blue "Generate Code" button.

*Published documents can still be edited. After editing, they will need to be republished.
