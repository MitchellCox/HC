To create a double-ended arrow from a line:

  1. Drag a line from a shape or create a freestanding line by pressing [L] on your keyboard.
  2. Select the line and go to the top properties bar. You should see a bunch of styling options for lines, including two drop down menus for line endings (see below). One menu probably says "None" and the other has an arrowhead type selected.
  3. Change those to alter the endings of the line.
