Easily customize the way a shape looks by changing the shape size, shape color, border color, or border thickness. This tutorial will also explain how to lock your shapes to the canvas.

Change the shape size
  1. Select the shape by single-clicking on it.
  2. Grab a corner of the shape and drag to resize it. You can also change the width and height in the Metrics panel to the right.

Change the border thickness
  1. Select the shape by single-clicking on it.
  2. Adjust the line thickness in the Properties bar or in the Graphic panel.

Changing the shape color

Option #1:

  1. Select the shape by single-clicking on it.
  2. Click on the fill color box on the Properties bar to open the color picker OR Open the Graphic panel from the dock, and click the color box under “Fill.”

Select the color you want, and Lucidchart will recolor the shape.

Change the border color
  1. Select the shape.
  2. Click on the line color box on the Properties bar to open the color picker OR Open the Graphic panel in the dock, and click the color preview under “Line.”
  3. Select the color you want, and Lucidshape will recolor the border.

Apply gradients

To apply a simple gradient:

  1. Select the shape by single-clicking on it.
  2. Click the “Fill” box on the Properties bar.
  3. Select the gradient circle at the bottom of the panel. The Graphic panel appears and fills the object with a simple gradient with a light tone of the existing color.
  4. Select the angle of the gradient and its colors under the “Simple Gradient” option.

To apply an advanced gradient:

  1. For even more control over your gradient, select “Advanced Gradient” from the “Fill” menu in the Graphic panel.
  2. Add and modify colors in the gradient bar. Click anywhere along the bar to add more colors and slide markets to change the intensity.
  3. Modify the gradient using the sliders. Click and drag a slider left or right to adjust color location. Single-click a slider to adjust its color. Click anywhere below the gradient bar to add an additional color stop. You can add multiple color stops.
  4. Choose between a radial or linear gradient and change its angle.

Change Shape
  1. To change the style of the shape in the diagram, right-click on the shape.
  2. Select “Change Shape” from the menu.
  3. Choose the new shape you wish to use from the list of shapes.

Lock shape to canvas
  1. Select the shape by single-clicking on it.
  2. Click the lock symbol on the Properties bar.
