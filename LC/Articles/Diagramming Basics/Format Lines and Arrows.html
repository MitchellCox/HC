Easily customize how lines look by changing color, thickness, style, angle, and arrow types.

Line Color

To change the line color, follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. Click on the line color box on the properties bar to open the color picker
  3. Select the color you want and it will recolor the line
Line Thickness

To change line thickness, follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. Click on the arrows in the properties bar to change the line width
    or
  3. Click on the arrows in the Graphic panel to change the line width
Line Style

To change line style, follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. Click on one of the three styles available (solid, dashed, or dotted) in the properties bar
    or
  3. Click on one of the three styles available (solid, dashed, or dotted) in the Graphic panel

You can also create and apply custom themes with your preferred line style. 

Line Shape

To change the shape of your line, follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. Click on one of the three angle types available (curved, elbow, direct) in the properties bar
Arrow Type

To change a line's arrow type, follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. Click on one of the arrow types (or 'None') for each side of the line
Special Cases: ERD and UML Lines

To add multiplicities or reverse line direction for ERD & UML lines follow these steps:

  1. Select the line by single-clicking on it
  2. A pop-up context panel appears giving you two options: Add Multiplicities and Reverse line direction
  3. After selecting the line, go to the properties bar to change the line endings. You'll notice that certain shape libraries have additional line endings that are common for those shapes.
