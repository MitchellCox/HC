Lucidchart has several collaboration tools that allow you and your team to work together effectively on documents. A summary of our most useful collaboration tools are below. (Note: If you would like to learn how to publish your document so non-lucidchart users can see it, click here).

Share your Document

Follow the steps below to share your document with other Lucidchart Users:

  1. Open the Lucidchart Document you would like to share.
  2. Click the orange share button in the top-right corner of your screen.
  3. Type in the email address(es) and select the desired permissions.
  4. Click the blue "Share Button"

Note: Your collaborators will see the shared document in their "Shared with Me" Folder.

Share your Folder

Sharing a folder with collaborators will give them access to every document within that folder. To do this, follow the steps below:

  1. Log into Lucidchart and stay on the documents tab, where you can see the grid of all your documents and folders.
  2. Hover your mouse over the desired folder and click "Share"
  3. Type in the email address(es) and set the desired permissions.
  4. Click the blue "Share" button

Note: You can also share documents this way. 

Real-Time Collaboration

If you share your document with a collaborator and give them "edit" access, you can both edit the document at the same time. Changes on one account will be reflected on the other's computer. To see this in action, see below:

Chat

The chat feature allows you to talk to your collaborator(s) while you're online and editing the document. To access this, click the "Chat" button near the bottom of the right toolbar. You can also start a live Video Hangout, simply click the green "Start a Hangout" button from the chat menu. 

Warning: The chat messages aren't stored, so be sure you save the important messages elsewhere before closing the document your collaborating on!

Commenting

If you and your collaborator(s) aren't working on the document at the same time, or you would like to store edits on the document, the commenting feature can be very useful. You can attach comments to a specific shape by clicking the shape>selecting "Comment" from the right toolbar>and clicking "+New Comment". You can also add comments to the entire document by adding a comment while a shape is NOT selected. All comments in the document can be seen from the Comment Menu. 

You can mention other team members in comments by typing in the "@" sign followed by their name or email address. Collaborators can also reply to individual comments.
