Lucidchart integrates with HipChat to make ad-hoc collaboration with Lucidchart more accessible via HipChat’s messaging platform. This article covers several main topics and features:

  1. Global vs. Room integration
  2. Installation steps
  3. Creating Lucidchart diagrams in HipChat Rooms
  4. Share to HipChat from Lucidchart
  5. Glance
Important note on Global Installation

For full functionality, Lucidchart must be installed globally when prompted and linked to a Team or Enterprise Lucidchart account. All free and paid users within that team will have full access to the integration.

Global installations allow users to create Lucidchart diagrams in any room, and also allows users to post any diagram back to HipChat from Lucidchart’s Share dialog. Installations to specific HipChat Room(s) allow creation of diagrams from HipChat within those Rooms, but not allow users to post diagrams from Lucidchart to HipChat.

To Install
  1. Click through to Get It Now.
  2. Install to Global (all rooms)

  3. Install the integration by granting permission.

  4. Grant HipChat access to your Lucidchart account.
  5. Link the integration to your Lucidchart team account.

Note that a Team or Enterprise account is required for full functionality (share back to HipChat, centralized admin / storage of diagrams, etc.)

Creating Lucidchart diagrams in HipChat Rooms

There are two ways to quickly create a Lucidchart diagram that can be collaboratively edited by anyone in the room. This is great for remote whiteboarding and digital brainstorming.

/lucidchart command

HipChat users can now use the /lucidchart command to instantly create and share diagrams for quick and easy collaboration.

Just type /lucidchart in any HipChat channel, and we'll post a link in that channel to a new diagram that anyone on your team can edit.

Attach "+" Lucidchart diagrams in the message box

Alternatively, you can click “+” to create a Lucidchart diagram. HipChat users can now use the /lucidchart command to instantly create and share diagrams for quick and easy collaboration.

Share to HipChat

Users can share their diagrams with HipChat Rooms from Lucidchart's Share dialog. This functionality exists for Lucidchart Team and Enterprise accounts where the HipChat add-on has been installed globally.

  1. Open up the Lucidchart “Share” dialog.

  2. Type in “HipChat” for a list of available HipChat rooms to share the diagram.

Glance

Glance allows users to easily see all Lucidchart diagrams that have ever been created or shared to a room without scrolling through the entire chat history or going to search. Diagrams can be pinned to ensure they always stay on top for reference. This is particularly valuable for frequently referenced diagrams like support escalation processes, architecture diagram, project plans, etc.

If you installed Lucidchart before March 7, 2016, you will need to remove and re-install Lucidchart in your HipChat instance.

To access previously posted diagrams

  1. Look at the Glance bar area in the upper right side of the HipChat page. You should see how many diagrams have been shared to the room.

  2. After selecting "Diagrams", you will see a list of diagrams created. Click on any of these to open it up in Lucidchart. You may pin a diagram to the top of the list by toggling the "Star" icon
