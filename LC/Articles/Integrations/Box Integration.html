The Lucidchart Box integration allows you to open your Lucidchart diagrams directly from your Box account.

Please note: This feature is only available on Enterprise accounts.

To install the integration:

  1. Go to your Account Settings page.
  2. On the finger tabs along the left side, select the option entitled “Box.”

  3. From there, click to link your Lucidchart account to your Box account. Here, if you haven’t already, you will need to log into Box and then confirm.

  4. After you have integrated, you may have to sync some documents to your Box account. Once that is completed, you will have a setting to unlink your account from Box if you wish.



In Box, you will see a “Lucidchart” folder. Once you open that folder, you can right-click > More Actions > Open with Lucidchart to open up your document and continue editing.
