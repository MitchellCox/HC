Lucidchart allows you to easily present your diagram by integrating with Google Slides. This will allow you to add other content and context to your Google presentation without affecting your Lucid diagram.

Get Started
  1. To get started, you will need to integrate with Google Drive. Integration with Google Slides will not work until you are integrated with Google Drive.
  2. Create your presentation using our Presentation Mode feature.
  3. Press the “Send to Google Slides” button on the bottom right corner of the screen.

  4. Wait for the presentation to be exported. This process could take a while so please be patient.
  5. Once the export is successful, click the “Edit it now!” link to be directed to your Google Slide presentation.

        
Slide and Presentation Titles

The first slide of your Google presentation will automatically become an editable title page displaying the original name of your Lucidchart document. Each additional slide in the presentation will inherit the slide’s title from its title in Lucidchart.

Troubleshooting

If you experience issues exporting your Lucidchart diagram to Google Slides, try the following steps:

  1. You signed in with your Google account when prompted.
  2. You clicked “Allow” when prompted to give Google Slides access to your documents.
  3. You have a strong internet connection.

If you continue to experience issues, try unlinking from Google Drive and then relinking.
