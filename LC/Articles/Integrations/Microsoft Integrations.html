Lucidchart integrates with Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint to make inserting diagrams directly into your Microsoft projects quick and easy. See below for specific instructions.

Word Integration

If you're using Microsoft Word 2013 or Word 2016, you can seamlessly insert your documents into Word through Apps for Office.

To install Lucidchart in Microsoft Word, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the “Apps for Office” button found in the Insert tab of the ribbon
  2. Locate Lucidchart in the App Store
  3. Click the “Add” button

If you're using a pre-2013 version of Microsoft Office, you can insert your diagram by first exporting it as an image, and then inserting the image into MS Word.

Excel Integration

If you're using Microsoft Excel 2013 or Word 2016, you can seamlessly insert your documents into Excel through Apps for Office. Watch this brief tutorial video to learn more.

To install Lucidchart in Microsoft Excel 2013 or 2016:

  1. Click on the “Apps for Office” button found in the Insert tab of the ribbon
  2. Locate Lucidchart in the App Store
  3. Click the “Add” button
PowerPoint Integration

Lucidchart's Add-in for Microsoft PowerPoint 2016 provides a quick and easy way to access, edit, and insert your diagrams into a PowerPoint slide deck.

With this add-in, you'll be able to walk through your Lucidchart diagram, including presentation frames and interactivity, within your PowerPoint presentation and then continue showing the rest of your PowerPoint slide deck to your co-workers.

Access the Add-In:

  1. Open Microsoft PowerPoint 2016 or 365.
  2. Once you're in the PowerPoint file, go to the "Insert" menu on the top bar and select "My Add-ins" under the Store option.
  3. Search the Store for "Lucidchart".
  4. Once you've located the "Lucidchart" app, click to open.
  5. A login screen will appear on your PowerPoint slide. Log in or, if you don't have an account, register.
  6. Once you've logged in, you'll be placed on your Documents Page, where you can create new documents and folders by using the buttons along the top of the page.
     

Insert a Lucidchart document:

  1. Once you've opened your Documents Page, you can click to create a new document (+Document), or you can hover over a document, click the More menu and "Open". 
  2. To edit, a new window will open. 
  3. The "save and close" button on the top of the document editor is an easy way to return to PowerPoint.
  4. When you're ready to insert a document, from your Documents List view, simply hover over the file and click "Select".
  5. The document will appear in your PowerPoint slide. You can use the controls on the bottom of the inserted document to zoom in and out or even to step through your Lucidchart presentation frames, if you have those set up.
  6. When you enter PowerPoint Presentation Mode, you will be able to view your Lucidchart document, zoom in/out, click through the presentation frames, and use any interactivity on the Lucidchart document.

Notes:

  • The diagram you insert is directly connected to your Lucidchart account. You must be connected to the Internet to be able to edit the diagram or insert a diagram.
  • Use the arrow on the top right of the inserted Lucidchart document to reload the document.
  • If you're having trouble getting the add-in to load, try uninstalling and reinstalling the add-in.
