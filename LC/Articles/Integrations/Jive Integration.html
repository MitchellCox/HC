To integrate Lucidchart with Jive, Jive Admins should install the add-on on the instance, which will allow users to find Lucidchart in the available apps.

Open the menu and go to the Add-Ons page. Choose Lucidchart from the list of available add-ons and click Install.

If users want to be added to a paid Lucidchart Team subscription when they use the add-on, you will need to copy and paste Jive's Client ID & Secret into Lucidchart, and connect to Jive.

Click the "gear" icon to open the menu for the add-on, and View Client ID and Secret.

Open a new window and log into your Lucidchart Admin account. Go to Team Admin > App Integration > Jive, and copy and paste the Jive Client ID and Secret into the fields.

Make sure to connect to your Jive instance. Feel free to close the window and go back to Jive.

Once installed, any Jive user on the instance can use the Lucidchart add-on to create a new document.

Open the Create menu, and create a new Jive Document, or visit an existing Jive document.

Use a Bang! App and start typing Lucidchart to insert a diagram. Log into your Lucidchart account, then choose a diagram, or create one. Your selection will be inserted.

Click on the image to load it in the viewer.

Use the gray controls bar on the Viewer to make updates and change viewing preferences.
