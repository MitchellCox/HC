You may be wondering how to use or make the most of the team management pages in Lucidchart. In this tutorial, we’ll go over the most commonly used functions of the Users section of the Admin Dashboard. Only Account Owners or designated Team Admins of a Team or Enterprise account can access the Admin Dashboard.

Create a Team
  1. If you have one started a team yet, visit our pricing page and start a Team trial. You can also visit your Subscription Level page and select a team size for purchase.

  2. Once you’ve started a Team trial or subscription, visit the Team page in your account and click “Create Team.” After clicking, you’ll see the Admin Dashboard.

Add Users

After creating your team, you’ll need to add users to fill the license seats you purchased. Follow these steps to add users:

  1. Click on the “Users” tile. You’ll see the following page with several notable features:
  • The licenses meter at the bottom left
  • The edit pencil next to your team name (this is where you create groups)
  • Standard filters for Product and User views
  • The user list in the center of the page, with a gray “Actions” button, a red “Pending Requests” button, and a gray “Users” button
  • An Information bar on the right for viewing profile information about a selected user (none selected in the example below)

  1. Click the “+ Users” button to manually add users. You’ll see your number of available licenses, the fields for entering user information, and the license setting that will apply to this user creation effort. If the user already has an account, he or she will receive an automated email invitation to join your team.

  2. Click “Add to Team” to see the results of your action and the total number of users invited or added.

  3. If you want to create or invite more than one user at a time, create a CSV and upload a list of user details. Click “CSV Upload” to switch to this view and read through our suggestions for formatting the CSV. We will search for and note any erroneous fields when you pull in the CSV, so take a look and correct those fields before uploading your data.

If the user already has an account, he or she will receive an automated email invitation to join your team.

Add Groups to the Team
  1. After creating users, go back to the Users page and click the edit pencil next to your team name. Select “Create Subgroup” to add the group.

  2. After creating a subgroup, select a minimum of one user and click the gray “Actions” button above the center user list. Select “Edit Group” and click the + or X button to manage the users in a listed group.



Edit User Profile, Licenses, Roles, Groups
  1. After adding a user to a group, check out any of the other options in the Actions menu. You can edit a user profile,

     

    assign licenses,



    assign a role,



    and add or remove users from existing groups.

  2. Click the red “Pending Requests” button to check your pending invitations to join the team, and change your license allocation settings.
