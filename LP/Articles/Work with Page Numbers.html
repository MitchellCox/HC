This tutorial explains how to add page numbers in the header and footer of your Lucidpress document.

Adding Page Numbers to the Header
  1. Once your document is open, navigate your mouse to the “Document” panel on the right side of the canvas and click the check box next to “Header”.

  2. Navigate your mouse to the canvas and double click the header bar until it’s green.
  3. Next, click on “Insert” and then click on “Page Number”. This will automatically generate the page number for the current pages in your canvas and to all pages that are added after.

 

Adding Page Numbers to the Footer
  1. Once your document is open, navigate your mouse to the “Document” panel on the right side of the canvas and click the check box next to “Footer”.

  2. Navigate your mouse to the canvas and double click the footer bar until it’s green.
  3. Next, click on “Insert” and then click on “Page Number”. This will automatically generate the page number for the current pages in your canvas and to all pages that are added after.
