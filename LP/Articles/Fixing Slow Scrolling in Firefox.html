A browser-specific bug that is resulting in very slow scrolling of Lucidpress scroll areas has been reported by a number of users. If you experience this problem, you can resolved the issue by following the steps below:

  1. Open up the 'Preferences' menu in Firefox.
  2. Click on the 'Advanced' tab.
  3. Click on the 'General' subtab within the Advanced tab.
  4. Uncheck the checkbox next to 'Smooth scrolling'.
  5. The issue should be resolved.
