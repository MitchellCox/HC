In this tutorial, we'll give you a quick update and step-by-step directions to help you add links and interactivity in seconds! Keep reading to learn more.

 

We removed the vague "hotspot" objects, and added interactive objects called "buttons" to the Content Bar at the left.




To use a button:

  1. Drag a button shape to the page.
  2. Enter a URL, email, or page number in the right pane.

To add a link without using a button:

  1. Add a shape or text box to the page and look at the pane to the right. 

  2. Select the shape or text and go to the pane on the right. Click on the 'Link' option and choose how you want to link.



If you have any questions about these changes, please feel free to email support@lucidpress.com. We are more than happy to help!
