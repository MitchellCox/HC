Account levels and features

There are four different types of account levels on Lucidpress: Free, Basic, Professional, and Team. These account levels provide different features and storage amounts. You can start a trial on any of our paid subscription levels here without a credit card. Changing your account level

You can view your options to change your account level at any time by visiting the Subscription Level page of your account. You can choose to keep your current level of service, upgrade to a higher level, or downgrade to a lower level. If you downgrade your account, please remember that your access to your documents may be restricted due to fewer features or less storage space. 

There are options for toggling between monthly and annual pricing at the top left of the pricing page. Annual subscriptions are more cost effective in the long run, so check out the price breakdown before making a decision. Then, make sure to have the appropriate option chosen when subscribing.

Adding more storage space

You can check your storage space at any time by looking at the bottom right of your Documents page or by accessing your Storage Space page. The storage indicator specifies what is taking up your storage space. In the example below, 87% of the storage space is taken up by images and 13% is from documents.

If you run out of storage space, purchasing additional storage is easy. If you are on a Free or Basic account and have run out of storage space, simply upgrade to the next account level via the Subscription Level page.

To purchase more storage space while on a Professional or Team Account, follow these simple steps:

  1. On your Storage Space page, click the button to 'Add storage space'.
  2. This will then prompt you to choose how much additional space you'd like to add to your account. The pricing sheet also gives you the annual or monthly, depending on your subscription type, change in your payment. 
  3. After choosing your level, click 'Continue' on the bottom right.
  4. You will now be on the confirmation page, which allows you to review your selection and the payment change. Press 'Pay now' when you're ready to add more storage space.
