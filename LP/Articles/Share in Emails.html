Now, customers using email marketing tools like Constant Contact can send their Lucidpress document in their newsletters! Follow these steps to insert your document into the email.

Â 

In your Lucidpress document:

  1. Click the blue "Share" button.

  2. Click the "On the Web" option and follow the instructions to generate a published URL, or update an existing URL.

  3. After publishing, click the "In Email" option and select the desired thumbnail size, then click "Generate Code."



  4. Copy and paste the code into the HTML section of your email.




In your email client, (Ex. Constant Contact):

  1. Open your email, and click the little "pencil" icon for editing your text.

  2. Click the HTML button, and enter the generated code on the appropriate line.

  3. Your document will appear in a perfectly sized thumbnail, with the link to the published URL beneath the image. This is the thumbnail at "medium."
