This tutorial will show you how to change your page and document settings to match your vision for your document.

Pick the Page Size

The first thing to choose is your page size, so you will want to know what kind of page you need. You may choose from any of the following options. You can bring up the 'Document' tab on the right by clicking on the grey area outside of the canvas. You can also change the page size for one specific page by using the 'Page' pane. Select your desired page size or enter a custom size under 'Size & Orientation':

  • Tablet / Mobile / PC
  • Letter
  • Legal
  • Executive
  • A3
  • A4
  • A5
  • Tabloid
  • Folio
  • Statement
  • Custom
Portrait or Landscape
Decide whether your content will present best on a portrait or a landscape page. You can always go back and change this setting, but it is easier when you don't need to rearrange content.
 
Margins
You can change your margin size for the whole document in the 'Document' pane. You can also change the margin per page with the 'Page' pane. 
 
      
 
Spreads

If you are creating a magazine or book-style document, turn on 'Spreads' in the 'Document' pane. This will allow you to view pages side by side.

Background
Change the background fill to a solid color, a gradient, or an image by selecting the appropriate option from the drop-down menu. 
 
Header and Footer
To add a header or footer, pull up the 'Document' pane on the right by clicking the gray around your document and check the appropriate respective boxes.
 
Bleed
To set a bleed for your document, pull up the 'Document' pane as outlined above and click the arrow next to 'Margins'. For more information, please see this article.
