Notes

You can add a note to any shape in your diagram.

  • To add a note, right-click on the shape and select “Add Note”. Once you click out of the note, the note icon will appear outside the shape.

  • To view or edit the note, click on the note icon.

  • To delete the note, right click on the shape and select “Delete Note”.
  • To hide the notes in present mode, click the settings icon in the top right corner and deselect “Show Notes”.
