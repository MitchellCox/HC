Lucidchart allows you to easily create UML sequence markup diagrams from text. To enter your markup in Lucidchart:

  1. Click “Shapes” on the Shapes Toolbar.
  2. Drag and drop or pin the UML Sequence shape library to your Shape Toolbox.
  3. Click “Use Markup” in the UML Sequence library.

  4. Enter your text in the editor that appears.
  5. Select “Build” to generate your diagram.

If you need to change or add to your diagram, simply select the diagram and edit the markup in the editor that appears in the Shapes Toolbox. You can customize your sequence diagram the same way you would any other diagram.
