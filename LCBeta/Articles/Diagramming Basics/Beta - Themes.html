Lucidchart’s theme feature is helpful for consistently stylizing your diagram without having to recreate the same look over and over again. You can use one of our preset themes or create your own.

To use a preset theme:

  1. Click “Theme” from the Right Menu.
  2. Choose a preset theme. You can see how a theme looks on your diagram by hovering over the thumbnail.

To create your own theme:

  1. Style an object line with colors, gradients, borders, text, etc.
  2. Click “Theme” from the Right Menu.
  3. Select the styled object.
  4. Click “Set Default”.
  5. Click “Apply All” if you would like your new theme applied to all existing objects.
