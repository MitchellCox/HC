Adding comments makes it easy to collaborate in real-time with others working on the same document. You can add comments to specific shapes or to the document as a whole.

To comment on a shape:

  1. Select the shape on which you would like to comment.
  2. Choose “Comments” from the Right Menu or "Add Comment" from the Insert dropdown menu.
  3. Click “New Comment”.
  4. Type your comment. You can @mention a name or email to collaborate with others.

OR

  1. Right click the shape on which you would like to comment.
  2. Select “Add Comment”.
  3. Type your comment. You can @mention a name or email to collaborate with others.

To comment on the entire document:

  1. Choose “Comments” from the Right Menu or select "Add Comment" from the Insert dropdown menu.
  2. Make sure you do not have a specific shape selected, and choose “New Comment”.
  3. Type in your comment. You can @mention a name or email to collaborate with others.

To view entire comment thread:

  1. Choose “Comments” from the Right Menu.
  2. Click the comment you would like to view.
  3. Select the geotag icon to identify which shape the comment refers to.
  4. Select “People” to see who contributed the comment.
  5. Reply to the comment.
  6. Click “Resolve” to close the comment.

To view resolved comments:

  1. Choose “Comments” from the Right Menu.
  2. Click the dropdown at the top of the Right Menu currently titled “Active”.
  3. Change the filter by selecting “Resolved”.

You will see notifications on the comments symbol in the Right Menu whenever someone makes a comment on a document on which you are a collaborator.
