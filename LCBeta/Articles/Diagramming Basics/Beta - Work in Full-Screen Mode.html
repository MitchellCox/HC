Get more out of your Lucidchart experience by taking advantage of our full-screen functionality. In full-screen mode, only the canvas will be visible. To enter full-screen mode, click the full-screen mode icon in the top right corner of the Options Bar.

  • If you click the icon once, you will enter full-screen mode with the Options Bar and File Menu still visible. To view the Toolbox or Right Menu, simply hover over the appropriate area.
  • If you double click the icon, the Options Bar will disappear as well. You can view any of the menus by hovering over the appropriate area.
  • To exit full-screen mode, click the icon.
