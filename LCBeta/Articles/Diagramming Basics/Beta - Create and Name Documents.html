Start from scratch or select a template from our collection -- creating a new document is simple, whether you are inside or outside the editor.

To create from the Documents list:

  1. Choose the orange “+ Document” button to create a blank diagram.

  2. Click the dropdown menu next to the “+ Document” button to select a template. Browse the categories on the right to help you find a specific template - each has unique features to make diagramming simple and efficient.

To create from inside the Editor:

  1. Select "File" from the File Menu.
  2. Choose “+New Document”.
  3. Choose a blank diagram or one of the standard templates. Browse the categories on the right to help you find a specific template - each has unique features to make diagramming simple and efficient.

To name a document from inside the Editor:

  1. Click on the current document title (located in the middle of the File Menu).
  2. Enter the new title.

To name a document from the Document’s List:

  1. Right-click on a thumbnail and choose “rename.”
  2. Enter the new name.

OR

  1. Click on the ellipsis on the thumbnail.
  2. Select “Rename”.
  3. Enter the new title.
