Create a Document:

  1. Choose “+ Document”. You can choose a blank diagram or a template.

  2. Drag out shapes and lines to start creating your diagram.
  3. For more shape libraries, click “Shapes” and pin the libraries you would like to view in the Toolbox.

Insert Shapes and Lines:

  1. To insert objects, choose a shape from the Toolbox and drag and drop it onto the canvas.
  2. You can add text either by immediately typing after you create a new shape or by double clicking on the shape to add or edit text.
  3. To draw lines, (1) click one of the four red circles around the block to automatically create a line and be prompted to select a shape, (2) click and drag from one of the four red circles, (3) click and drag from anywhere on the shape’s border, (4) drag the line shape from the Toolbox, (5) Right click on the canvas and select “Draw Line”.

Insert an Image:

  1. Click the image icon at the top of the Shape Toolbox and select an image to import.

  2. The image will be placed directly on the canvas.
  3. To save the image for future use, drag it into a custom library.

OR

  1. Click “Shapes” > “Import” > “Image” and select an image to import.

  2. Select a custom library for the shape to be placed in and click “Import”.

OR

  1. Click the search icon in the Shape Toolbox.
  2. Select Google Images.
  3. Search an image.
  4. Drag and drop the desired image onto the canvas.
  5. To save the image for future use, drag it into a custom library.
