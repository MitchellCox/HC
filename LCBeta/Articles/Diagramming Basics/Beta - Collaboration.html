You can make diagramming a group experience by inviting collaborators to view, edit, or comment on your document, depending on the permission level you grant them. For more information on how to do this, click here.

The top right corner of the editor shows which collaborators are on a document at any given time. Each person will appear as a colored bar with an initial. When you hover over the bar, the full name of the collaborator will appear.

When someone is editing a shape, the shape will be outlined in the color assigned to that collaborator. If you hover over the shape, you can see their full name. If you are in view-only mode, you will not be able to see collaborators.
