Our drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to create diagrams.

  • To insert a shape, drag an object from the Toolbox and drop it on the canvas.

  • To add text, either begin typing immediately after creating the shape, or double-click on the shape to add or edit text.
  • To align shapes:
    1. Turn on “Grid Snap” in Page Settings, and choose a grid size. You can adjust the measurements under "Page". Standard grid size is 20 pixels.

    2. Select all the shapes you would like to align and click the Position icon in the Options Bar to choose from the alignment relationships listed at the bottom.
