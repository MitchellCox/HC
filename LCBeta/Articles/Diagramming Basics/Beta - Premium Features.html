Basic, Pro, Team, and Enterprise accounts unlock Lucidchart’s advanced functionality, meaning you have access to features that will take your diagramming to the next level. 

  • Advanced Shape Libraries: Make diagramming even easier with more shape libraries to choose from.
  • Unlimited Items: Free accounts are limited to just 60 objects per document, but when you upgrade, the sky is the limit.
  • Unlimited Documents: Free accounts are restricted to no more than five active documents -- upgrade and diagram to your heart’s content.
  • Edit Imported Documents: Keep all your diagrams in one place by uploading and editing Visio, Omnigraffle, and Gliffy files.
  • Presentation Mode: Create dynamic presentations directly in Lucidchart.
  • Revision History: For when you change your mind - view prior edits or switch to an earlier version of your document.
