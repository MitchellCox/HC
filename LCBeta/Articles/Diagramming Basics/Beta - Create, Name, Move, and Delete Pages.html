Within a single document, you can create multiple pages that are managed within the Page Drawer. The Page Drawer allows you to choose which page you would like to currently view. You can manage your pages either by opening the Page Drawer or by using the Page Tab along the bottom of the editor.

If you use the Page Tab, all your pages will be listed along the bottom of the editor. You can also see a list of your pages by clicking the list icon next to the Page Drawer.

To manage pages using the Page Tab:

  • Create pages by selecting the (+) icon.
  • Name pages by double-clicking on the existing title and typing, or right-clicking on the title and selecting "Rename".
  • Move pages by selecting the page title and dragging and dropping.
  • Delete pages by right-clicking the page title and clicking "Delete".

If you use the Page Drawer, open the Page Drawer by clicking this icon .

To create:

  1. Open the Page Drawer.
  2. Choose the large (+) to add a new page.

To name:

  1. Open the Page Drawer.
  2. Click on the existing name of a page and change the page title.

OR

  1. Right click on the page and choose “Rename”.

To move:

  1. Open the Page Drawer.
  2. Click and drag the page to its new location.

To delete:

  1. Open the Page Drawer.
  2. Hover over the page you wish to delete and click the “x” that appears in the top right corner.

OR

  1. Right click on the page and select “Delete”.

Master Pages:

With Lucidchart’s Master Pages, you can create a page with certain items, such as a header/footer, and apply it to other pages. Anytime you change the Master Page, it will be reflected across all the other pages to which you apply the Master Page.

To create a Master Page:

  1. Create a new page and add items you would like applied across multiple pages.
  2. Open the Page Drawer, right click on the page you created, and select “Convert to Master Page”.

OR

  1. Right-click the page title on the Page Tab and select "Convert to Master Page". The page title will become blue and an "M" symbol will appear.

To apply a Master Page:

  1. Create a new page.
  2. Open the Page Drawer, right click on the page you created, and select “Apply Master Page”.

OR

  1. Right-click the page title on the Page Tab and select "Apply Master Page".
