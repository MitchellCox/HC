The publish feature makes it easy to share documents with anyone on the web. You can publish your diagram to a unique Lucidchart web page or publish it to a permanent PDF link.

  1. Click “Share” from the Right Menu or from the F and select “Publish”. You can also click "Publish" from the File dropdown menu.
  2. Choose to publish the full document URL, full document PDF, a single page, or an area selection.
  3. Click “Generate Link” to show the URL you can copy and paste.
  4. If you choose to publish an area selection, choose the format and quality. You will be directed back to the canvas -- click and drag to select the area you would like published. Once you have done so, click the “Share” button again to access the published link.
