Google Chrome users can install our free application to enable offline document creation and editing so that even without Internet access, you can stay productive working on your diagrams. Lucidchart automatically switches to offline mode when you disconnect from the Internet and syncs your changes as soon as you come back online.

How to Download:

  1. Download the Google Chrome browser if you don’t have it already.
  2. Open the Chrome Web Store and download the Lucidchart Chrome App.

How to Use the Application:

  1. After downloading the app, sign into Lucidchart through the browser, either by clicking the “Launch app” in the Chrome Web store listing or clicking the Lucidchart icon from a new tab. Doing so will sync your Lucidchart files to your computer, and you only have to do this once.
  2. Now you can work offline, creating new documents and editing existing ones. Please note that some features, such as real-time collaboration and document download, are not accessible during offline mode.
  3. When you regain Internet access, or the next time you log into Lucidchart, all changes will be instantly saved to your account.
