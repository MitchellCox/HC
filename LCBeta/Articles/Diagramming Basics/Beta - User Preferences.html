User Preferences

You can access your user preferences by selecting "Account" in the top right corner of the editor.

  • Choose default settings for zoom, page size, and page units.
  • Choose a default setting for whether the grid is on or off.
  • Choose a default setting for whether line binding is manual or auto.
  • Choose a default setting for whether you would like to be prompted for the next block after drawing a line.
  • Choose whether or not to enable search for Lucidchart, IconFinder, and Google Images.
