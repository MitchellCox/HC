Embedding allows you to put your Lucidchart creations directly into your webpage, where they will be available with viewing options such as zoom functionality, full-screen mode, and the ability to look through the pages of a document.

  1. Click “Share” from the Right Menu or File dropdown menu.
  2. Select “Embed”.
  3. Choose a pre-set size for the diagram or create a custom size.
  4. Click “Activate Embed Code”.
  5. Copy and paste the generated html text directly into your website.
